About DJ Paloma

A Journey of Faith & Music

DJ Paloma is Ghana's premier Gospel DJ, dedicated to creating transformative praise and worship experiences that uplift spirits and bring communities together in faith.

With years of experience spinning at churches, conferences, and major gospel events across Ghana, DJ Paloma has become synonymous with excellence in gospel entertainment. His unique ability to blend contemporary gospel hits with timeless worship classics creates an atmosphere where hearts are touched and souls are stirred.

Mission

To use music as a powerful tool for worship, spreading the gospel message and creating unforgettable faith-driven experiences that glorify God.
